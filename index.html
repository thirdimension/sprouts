Our Work

Sprouts for Learning is an organization working to ensure everyone has access to quality education. By mobilizing volunteer groups across South Korea and partnering with local organizations in South Korea, China, India, Uganda, Pakistan, and Rwanda, Sprouts for Learning has helped 4000+ students across the globe increase their literacy and numeracy in fun ways such as worksheets, books, and games!
