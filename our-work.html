Our Work

Sprouts for Learning is an organization working to ensure everyone has quality education. By mobilizing volunteer groups across South Korea and partnering with local organizations in South Korea, China, India, Uganda, Pakistan, and Rwanda, Sprouts for Learning has helped 4000+ students across the globe increase their literacy and numeracy in fun ways such as worksheets, books, and games!

Tell me more!

Worksheet Booklets

Sprouts for Learning also helps make fun and colorful worksheets to help supplement students’ learning. The educational materials team creates the worksheets tailored to fit the organization’s and children’s needs. The worksheets cover a wide range of topics, from reading, writing, grammar, numeracy, algebra, and more!

Books

One of the most powerful and fun ways of learning English is through reading. Thus, Sprouts for Learning has created numerous illustrated books for students, covering a variety of topics. Sprouts for Learning has also illustrated several books on folktales from specific nations, to help students improve their reading skills and increase their cultural awareness.

Volunteer Groups

Currently, we have high school students who help teach elementary school children English in local libraries. The high school students prepare fun games, read children’s stories, prepare worksheets, and activities for students to learn English in an easy and fun way. The high school students also converse with the children in English, helping them to learn English in an authentic way.
